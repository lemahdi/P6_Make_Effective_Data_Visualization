Saint-Louis, Pittsburgh, Chicago .. What do they have in common?

These 3 cities are home to airports that have seen their visit-share change drastically. The graph on the left shows shares of US aiports attendance between years 1987 and 2008. With top 5 airports keeping their lead positions at almost the same ratios, most others have swung in a range between +-.05% and +-.8%. However, Saint-Louis and Pittsburgh airpots have recorded the largest decrease making their shares 2 to 4 times less than what they were in 1987, while Chicago airport jumped by 257% more share.
